Key Concepts

  • A portfolio allows non-players to appreciate game art and interactivity through visual documentation.
  • Use professional platforms like Behance to showcase the full design process from sketch to final build.
  • Avoid using internal course numbers in public-facing portfolios to ensure clarity for employers.
  • SketchFab integration allows for interactive, web-based 3D model viewing within a portfolio page.
  • Documenting modular design and process work demonstrates critical soft skills and human creativity.
  • Short, looping GIFs are more effective than long videos for showcasing specific animations or mechanics.

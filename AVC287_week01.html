Key Concepts

  • 10 Principles of Motion Design provide the foundation for all animation work
  • Keyframes mark start/end positions; the computer interpolates between them
  • Graph editor visualizes animation curves and controls timing precisely
  • Ease in = slow start (acceleration); ease out = slow end (deceleration)
  • Speed is controlled by keyframe spacing: closer = faster, farther = slower
  • Blender timeline shows keyframes as diamonds; graph editor shows curves

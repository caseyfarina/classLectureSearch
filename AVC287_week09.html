Key Concepts

  • Rigify provides pre-built professional rigs with advanced controls beyond basic manual rigging
  • Meta rigs are intermediate templates that generate final control rigs through the add-on
  • Never scale armatures directly as it breaks interdependent constraint relationships
  • Heel peel controllers enable realistic foot animation with toe-grounded movement
  • Counter-rotation between hips and chest creates natural body movement in walk cycles
  • Weight painting refinements are essential for proper head and neck deformation
  • Global space rotation works better for arm swinging in natural walking motion

Key Concepts

  • Opposite arm and leg movement is fundamental to natural walking
  • All animation tracks must be same length for proper cycling
  • Weight and mass communicated through hip compression and timing
  • Starting over often yields better results than fixing complex problems
  • Mixamo provides excellent motion capture reference for walk cycles
  • Rigify offers comprehensive character rigging but requires proper setup
  • Visual reference and motion paths help analyze animation quality
  • Practice and iteration are essential for mastering walk cycles

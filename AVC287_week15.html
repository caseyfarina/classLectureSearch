Key Concepts

  • The final portfolio consists of both a video reel and a professional project page.
  • SketchFab allows students to embed interactive 3D models and animations into web portfolios.
  • The glTF/GLB format is the primary choice for exporting Blender content for web-based viewers.
  • Professional portfolios should highlight the technical process, including drivers and graph editor work.
  • Demonstrating soft skills like communication and problem-solving is critical for industry hiring.
  • Process documentation serves as essential proof of human-authored work in an AI-saturated market.

Key Concepts

  • Mouth sprite sheet logic requires precise mathematical ratios for row and column mapping.
  • Grease pencil line weights behave differently in world space versus screen space during camera moves.
  • Professional audio syncing should be performed in a dedicated DAW to bypass frame-snapping limitations.
  • Animation reels must be sequenced with a strong hook and consistent visual momentum to engage employers.
  • Local space drivers are critical for ensuring character rigs function correctly when moved within a scene.
  • Effective lip sync focuses on hard consonants and vowel-specific mouth shapes rather than every letter.
  • Particle systems can be used to create stylized effects like fuses and sparks for Rube Goldberg machines.
